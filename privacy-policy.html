Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sleep Sounds: White Noise Mix ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

We do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Precise location
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Messages or chat data

Our app does not require user registration or account creation.

Information Collected by Third Parties

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to serve and personalize ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • App interaction data (ad views, clicks, and impressions)
  • Diagnostics data (crash logs, performance data)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

The app itself stores only local preferences on your device (such as timer settings and volume preferences) using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, please refer to:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • How Google Uses Data

Data Retention

We do not store any user data on external servers. All app preferences are stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app's target audience is users aged 13 and above.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All app data is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
